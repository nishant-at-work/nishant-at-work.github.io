Co-authored with Archana Singh. Published by Greenpeace India Society, Bengaluru, October 2024.

This study traced the impact of Delhi's free bus travel scheme for women --- the first of its kind in India --- on women's daily lives. Based on a survey of over 500 women and 20 in-depth interviews, the research found that the scheme significantly increased women's independent mobility, with about two-thirds reporting enhanced confidence to commute on their own, and 75% redirecting transport savings towards household needs and education.
