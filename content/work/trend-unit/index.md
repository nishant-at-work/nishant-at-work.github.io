I head the TREND Unit at Youth Ki Awaaz --- a cultural research and evidence-building programme working with young India through an unorthodox mix of research methods.

TREND runs [Yoot](https://trend.youthkiawaaz.com), a WhatsApp-based civic polling system reaching 15,000+ young Indians; [CHORUS](https://trend.youthkiawaaz.com), a discourse analysis framework drawing on 18 years and 12,000+ articles of citizen writing; and publishes research briefs on elections, data protection, AI, and civic life. We think of this work as building a commons of curiosity.
