Co-authored with A. Farooq and Amruta SN. Published by Greenpeace India Society, Bengaluru, 2024.

This study examines women's perspectives on public bus transport in Mumbai, documenting the barriers they face and the potential of fare-free schemes to improve access, safety, and participation in urban life.
