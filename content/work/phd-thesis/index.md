Doctoral thesis submitted to the Department of Civil Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, under the supervision of Prof. Geetam Tiwari and Prof. Reetika Khera.

The thesis develops new conceptual and analytical tools, grounded in the Capability Approach and critical theory, to understand and evaluate injustices in everyday urban mobility. Through a case study of North-West Delhi, it examines how structural inequalities shape people's ability to move through the city and access the opportunities that matter to them.
