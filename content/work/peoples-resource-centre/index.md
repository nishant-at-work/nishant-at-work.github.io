Between 2018 and 2021, I built and led the research and evidence-based advocacy programme at People's Resource Centre, a knowledge initiative working on social transformation in Indian cities. The programme spanned urban mobility, urban agriculture, urban rivers and riverfronts, democratising urban local governance, and climate justice.

Through this work, I organised national conferences including *Farming The City* (2020, 2021), co-produced research reports, and engaged policymakers, scholars, civil society organisations, and citizens in public consultations on urban policy questions.
