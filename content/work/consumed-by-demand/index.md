Co-authored with Rajendra Ravi. Published by People's Resource Centre, Delhi, 2019.

This monograph critically examines the rapid expansion of metro rail systems across Indian cities, questioning the assumptions driving these mega-investments and documenting their consequences for public finances and urban resources.
