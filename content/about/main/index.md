  I am an independent research consultant based in Delhi. I completed my PhD from the Department of Civil Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi in 2024. My thesis --- [*New Bearings on Justice in Urban Mobility*](/work/phd-thesis/) --- developed conceptual and analytical frameworks grounded in the Capability Approach and critical theory to understand and address injustices in everyday urban mobility.

  My research interests span the politics of transport planning, road safety, urban inequality, and mixed methodologies. I work across academic research, policy engagement, and public advocacy --- contributing to projects with the WHO, Greenpeace India, and community organisations.

  I currently head the [TREND Unit](https://trend.youthkiawaaz.com) (Research, Evidence and Narrative Development) at Youth Ki Awaaz --- a cultural research and evidence-building programme that uses polls, citizen discourse analysis, and narrative development to help young Indians make sense of their time. I also coordinate the [Public Transport Forum](https://ptf.neocities.org), a citizen collective advocating for free, safe, reliable, and inclusive public transport across Indian cities.

  Previously, I served as Project Scientist at the Transportation Research and Injury Prevention Centre (TRIP-C) at IIT Delhi, where I contributed to the *India Status Report on Road Safety: 2024*. From 2018 to 2021, I built and led the research and advocacy programme at People's Resource Centre, spanning urban mobility, agriculture, rivers, and democratic governance in Indian cities.

  I am a member of Sustainable Urban Mobility Network (SUM Net) India and Teachers Against Climate Crisis (TACC). My work on fare-free bus travel for women in Delhi and Mumbai has been featured in *The India Forum*, *Ideas for India*, *Down to Earth*, *Mongabay India*, and other publications.

  I hold a B.Tech.-M.Tech. dual degree from IIT Kanpur, where my master's thesis examined [school trip mode choice behaviour](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0966692317303277/) in Kanpur city.
